Sound Design

In this section, I'd like to showcase some of my composing and sound design work. I started focusing on sound design for video games after finishing Escape from Purrrgatory, since I already did the sound design and music for this project. It will be showcased in the Escape from Purrrgatory section.
Here, I want to focus on my other composing and sound work outside of projects. Following are some of my compositions as well as the sound design for an infinite jump mobile game.

Composition: Valley of Rivers


This composition features background ambience. It's composed for a scene or environment of a river running down a forest in the mountains, instilling a feeling of calm and hope.

Composition: Sorrow City


This composition features an entry section and is then supposed to be looped around the one minute mark. It's composed for a scene or environment of a postapocalyptic city amidst a vast wasteland.

Composition: Overgrowth


In this track, which is meant for a trailer of some sorts, I recorded everything on one guitar, including percussions, on multiple tracks. It was a fun exercise for creative creation of percussion.

Sound Design: Infinite Jump Game


For this Infinite Jump style game, I did music and sound design. The sound design was done using various objects and sounds created with my voice. It was my first sound design exercise after Escape from Purrrgatory.
